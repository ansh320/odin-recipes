Lasanga

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. The layers are assembled in a baking dish and baked until bubbly and golden. A hearty and flavorful dish, lasagna is popular worldwide and can be customized with various fillings like spinach, mushrooms, or seafood.

Lasagna is made by layering cooked lasagna sheets with a rich meat sauce (made from ground meat, tomato sauce, and spices) and creamy béchamel sauce. The layers are topped with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 40 minutes until bubbly and golden. Rest for 10 minutes before serving.

for meat sauce

  • ground beef(a mix of beef and pork)-about 450 gm
  • onion-1 medium,finely chopped
  • garlic 2,3
  • canned crushed tomatoes
  • tomato paste
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper
  • dried oregano and basil
  • red chilli flakes
  • sugar

steps to make classic lasgana

  1. heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat
  2. Sauté chopped onions until translucent (about 5 minutes).
  3. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon
  5. Stir in tomato paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, pepper, and sugar. Simmer the sauce gently for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Mix ricotta cheese, beaten egg, half of Parmesan cheese, and parsley/basil in a bowl.
  8. Boil lasagna noodles in salted water until al dente (if not using no-boil noodles). Drain and lay flat to prevent sticking.
  9. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  10. Grease baking dish with butter or oil
  11. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of the dish.
  12. Place a layer of noodles on top of the sauce.
  13. Spread a layer of ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  14. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the ricotta layer.Repeat layers (meat sauce → noodles → ricotta mixture → mozzarella) until ingredients are used up
  15. Top with noodles, meat sauce, mozzarella, and remaining Parmesan cheese.Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  16. Remove foil and bake for another 20-25 minutes until bubbly and golden on top.
  17. Let lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before cutting and serving.
